## Direction Options

### 1. Dark Museum (closest to current aesthetic)
**Reference:** UNESCO Virtual Museum of Stolen Cultural Objects — https://museum.unesco.org
- Near-black background + warm amber point lighting
- Artifacts emerge from darkness in semi-lit alcoves
- 3D spatial navigation, AAA game-engine feel
- Built by makemepulse using custom WebGL engine

### 2. Shadow / Chiaroscuro
**Reference:** Art Here 2025 "Shadows" (Louvre Abu Dhabi) — https://artprize-shadows.com
- Extreme dark background, subjects revealed from shadow
- Dramatic chiaroscuro lighting (Awwwards SOTD)
- Elegant serif display typography
- "Science emerging from darkness" narrative potential

### 3. Natural Science / Documentary
**Reference:** Microsoft × NHM "Visions of Nature" — https://unlocked.microsoft.com/nhm-visions-of-nature/
- Deep forest green / near-black + bioluminescent particle effects
- Nature documentary color grading
- Holographic animals in dark environments
- Fits well with animal motion research theme

### 4. Cyberpunk / Sci-Fi
**Reference:** Active Theory Studio — https://activetheory.net
- Pure black (#0B0B0B) + violet neon accents (#A970FF)
- Film-cut scene transitions, heavy bloom post-processing
- Monospace console typography

**Reference:** Samsy Ninja (WebGPU portfolio) — https://samsy.ninja
- Cyberpunk city, cyan/magenta neon, wet reflective floors
- Three.js + WebGPU, Awwwards SOTD Oct 2025
- Real-time multiplayer avatars

### 5. Minimal White Gallery (inverse reference)
**Reference:** Ruinart Unconventional Gallery — https://unconventionalgallery.ruinart.com
- Pure white walls + strong spotlights
- Awwwards SOTM Jan 2022
- High contrast, clinical cleanliness

## Common Patterns Across Dark/Cinematic Sites
- Background: `#050505`–`#111`, one warm accent (amber, violet, or cyan)
- Lighting: dramatic spotlights with baked lightmaps, bloom on all light sources
- Typography: high-contrast white/ivory, large display serif for headings
- Navigation: spatial 3D movement rather than traditional menus
- Transitions: film-cut style, not CSS page transitions
- Audio: ambient sound tied to environment zones
